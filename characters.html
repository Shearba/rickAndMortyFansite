Character Information

Rick Sanchez

Courtesy of www.adultswim.com

Rick Sanchez is not only the smartest person in the world, but the smartest person in every single dimension. A master of science and mischivious activities Rick is one of the titular characters because he and Morty travel on numerous adventures together using Rick's genius to cause all sorts of good and evil. Rick is the grandfather of Morty and Summer and Father and Father in-law respectiviely of Beth and Jerry.

Morty Smith

Courtesy of www.adultswim.com

Morty Smith is the second of the two titular characters in the show and is the grandson of Rick. Morty is a young boy in his earlier years of high school who isn't very popular in school. Morty has a lot of respect and admiration for his grandpa so he helps Rick by helping him on his adventures. Morty is also the son of Beth and Jerry, and brother of Summer.

Beth Smith

Courtesy of www.adultswim.com

Beth is the daughter of Rick and has the emotional baggage that goes along with it. Beth lost her mom at somewhat of an early age and when she was young as well her father disappered for years. Beth now works as a horse surgeon and hates how she is not as respected as a "real suregon". Beth is also the mother of Morty and Summer and wife of Jerry.

Jerry Smith

Courtesy of www.adultswim.com

Jerry is unsucessful in every aspect of his life. Despite his loveable charm Jerry has a hard time holding onto a job and an equally hard time holding on to his marriage. Unfortunately at the current point in the show Jerry is currently divorced and jobless and living in a motel. Jerry is the father of Morty and Summer and ex-husband of Beth.

Summer Smith

Courtesy of www.adultswim.com

Summer Smith is the older sister of Morty and she does not let him forget it. She is obsessed with being popular in school yet shows an angsty disposition to just about everything. Summer shows a lot of character progression throughout the show and at this point has a much more friendly view of her family. Summer is also the daugther of Beth and Jerry.

Jessica

Courtesy of www.adultswim.com

At Morty's high school Jessica is the coolest girl in the school. At the beginng of the show Jessica was merely the girl that Morty had fantasies about, but as the show has progressed she has become more of a promenient chracter who even has acompanied the main characters on a couple of psuedo-adventures.
